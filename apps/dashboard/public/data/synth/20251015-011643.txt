**Description**: Use a modern JavaScript framework (like React, Vue.js, or Angular) to build a single-page application that incorporates all functionalities in a cohesive manner.

Each approach has its own strengths and weaknesses, and the choice largely depends on project requirements, team expertise, and long-term maintenance considerations.
