## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Phase Definitions
- **Phase 1 (Weeks 1-8):** ✅ Foundation & Launch Readiness - **COMPLETE**
- **Phase 2 (Weeks 9-20):** 🎯 Enterprise Ready - **CURRENT**
- **Phase 3 (Q2 2026+):** 🚀 Scale & Innovate - **FUTURE**

### Sprint Structure
- **Duration:** 2 weeks per sprint
- **Ceremonies:** Planning (Day 1), Standups (Daily), Demo + Retro (Last day)
- **Velocity:** ~25-30 story points per sprint

### Success Metrics
- **Performance:** <2s load, <500ms API, <100ms search
- **Quality:** 80% coverage, 99.9% uptime
- **Security:** 0 high/critical vulnerabilities
